## Overview

TransportePro is a digital platform designed to optimize the management and hiring of freight and passenger transportation services. The website offers a seamless and efficient experience for both customers and administrators, enabling service quotes, booking management, and real-time information. Visit the live site at [transportepro.vercel.app](https://transportepro.vercel.app).

## Key Features

- **Instant Quote System**: Implemented a feature that allows customers to get estimated prices based on destination and service type.  
- **Booking and Tracking**: Developed a module to manage reservations and provide real-time updates on shipments or trips.  
- **Admin Dashboard**: Created an intuitive dashboard for managing fleets, customers, and service requests.  
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for a smooth experience across mobile and desktop devices.  
- **Local SEO Optimization**: Implemented SEO strategies to enhance visibility in local searches.  

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: For fast and optimized development.  
- **React**: To create an interactive and user-friendly interface.  
- **TypeScript**: For greater code stability and maintainability.  
- **TailwindCSS**: For a modern, responsive design.  
- **Prisma & PostgreSQL**: To efficiently manage customer, booking, and fleet data.  

## Challenges and Learnings

One of the biggest challenges was developing a real-time quoting system with multiple variables while maintaining high performance. Additionally, user experience optimization was key to simplifying the booking process and improving customer conversion rates.

## Outcome

The platform has significantly improved TransportePro's operational efficiency, reducing management time and increasing customer satisfaction. The implementation of an intuitive design and a simplified booking system has attracted more customers and enhanced visibility in the transportation sector.
