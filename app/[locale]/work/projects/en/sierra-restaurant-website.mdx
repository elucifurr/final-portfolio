## Overview

Restaurante Sierra is a local restaurant that needed a digital presence to attract more customers and simplify menu browsing and reservations. The website provides an intuitive navigation experience with essential information about the menu, opening hours, and location, all wrapped in a visually appealing and mobile-friendly design. Visit the live site at [restaurantesierra.vercel.app](https://restaurantesierra.vercel.app).

## Key Features

- **Digital Menu**: Designed an interactive display of the menu with images and dish descriptions.  
- **Online Reservations**: Implemented a section for easy customer reservations.  
- **Location & Contact**: Integrated Google Maps and direct contact buttons for improved accessibility.  
- **Local SEO Optimization**: Enhanced the site's structure for better ranking in local searches.  
- **Aesthetic Visual Design**: Applied colors and typography aligned with the restaurant's identity.  

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: For fast loading and excellent SEO optimization.  
- **React**: To deliver an interactive and engaging user experience.  
- **TailwindCSS**: For a clean, modern, and responsive design.  
- **ShadCN**: For a consistent and sleek UI.
